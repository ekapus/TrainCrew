About This App

I have long wanted to use technology to improve the immersiveness of model railroad operations.

This app started with the ideas from Lance Mindheim's "How To Operate a Modern Switching Layout" book. I then found a great example in the PDC BNML Conductors App, which has been extended and genericized.

This app has been developed by Ed Kapuscinski, owner of Conrail1285.com and is released under the GPL. It is available for download on Github.
